about me

I was born in 1984, in the eastern part of flanders.
I studied Modern Languages and Sciences in high school in Turnhout, after which I started studies on European Politics at the University of Maastricht. These studies were a firm struggle for me, as it was not my passion at all. That's why my career path might look kind of a labyrinth. But please read more about my career on the career-page.
Today I'm living in Overijse, one of the most beautiful places in flanders for cyclists and for grape lovers. I'm a Lean Professional at Colruyt Group, like to play football and love to watch cyclism. Especially the flanders classics. When I am not working I spend most of my time with my family, sometimes in the garden, otherwhise playing a board game. And like most of use, I spend my evenings, seated for netflix.
