The characteristics of this design

  • Colors: White, black and grey, with a focus on a white background to create zen.
  • Hints of blue to bring some colour in
  • Using bold to emphasize text, but no underlining.
  • Not to much specialities and distractions.
  • Showing html- and css-skills only when they are really functional and fiting the design.
